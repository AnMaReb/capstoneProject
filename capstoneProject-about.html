About Me

Some of my favorite ways to spend time are hanging out with animals, creating art, and being active.

The animals in my life are mainly dogs -- my own and my family's. There are many more than are pictured here, but this is a good overview.

The type of art I create these days includes painting, crochet, and wood burning.

Exercise is vital for both physical and mental health. The photos here are of me in my local gym, but it's also important and fun to be active in other ways outside of the gym. I walk my dog almost daily. Hiking is another way I love to stay active and get out into nature.
